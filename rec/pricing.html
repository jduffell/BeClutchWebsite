Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge transaction fees or service fees?

No. We charge one flat annual subscription fee. We never take a percentage of your registration revenue or charge extra per transaction. We keep your budget predictable.

What happens if my organization grows past my current plan's registration limit?

We believe in partnership. We will contact you to discuss moving to the next tier for your next contract cycle. We will never cut off service or charge unexpected fees.

How long does it take to implement BeClutch?

Our implementation time is 1 week. We handle the heavy lifting for you.

Do you have unlimited SMS messages as part of your annual fee?

We provide an SMS interface as part of all our tiers. Over 1,000 SMS messages requires additional billing from a 3rd party partner
