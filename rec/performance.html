Frequently Asked Questions

What is "uptime" and why is it important?

Uptime is the measure of time a system is successfully operational. Our 99.99% uptime guarantee means that our system is available and running virtually all the time, ensuring that your registration portal is always accessible to your community when they need it.

Our current system crashes every year. How is BeClutch different?

Many older systems are built on a single server with limited capacity. BeClutch is built on a modern, elastic cloud infrastructure. This means that as more users visit the site, our system automatically adds more resources to handle the load. This prevents the overloads that cause crashes.

Is our community's personal information safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all data transmitted between the user's browser and our servers. All sensitive data is encrypted at rest in our database. We adhere to strict security protocols to protect all personal information.

Do we need our own IT department to manage BeClutch?

Not at all. BeClutch is a fully managed, software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform. We handle all the technical aspects—security, updates, backups, and performance monitoring. You simply log in and use the software.
